This guide covers Desktop, iOS, and Android. Where the experience differs, platform-specific instructions are noted.

You can export your data from the hotline at any time. Exports are available for notes, contacts, reports, and call history.

## Exporting your notes

Go to the **Notes** section and look for the export option. Your notes are exported as an encrypted file that only you can read. Even if the export file is intercepted or accidentally shared, the contents remain protected.

## Exporting call history

Admins can export call history from the **Calls** section. The export includes:

- Call dates and times
- Duration
- Status (answered, missed, voicemail)
- Which user handled each call

The export is formatted as JSON, suitable for record-keeping or analysis.

## Exporting contacts

Admins can export the contact directory from the **Contacts** section. Contact data is exported in its encrypted form to maintain privacy protections.

## Exporting reports

Reports can be exported individually or in bulk. The exported data includes the report content, thread replies, and metadata.

## GDPR data requests

If your organization operates under European data protection rules (GDPR), individuals have the right to request a copy of their personal data. As an admin, you can fulfill these requests by:

1. Searching for the person's data across calls, contacts, and reports
2. Exporting the relevant records
3. Providing the export to the requester

All personal data is identifiable and exportable.

## Bulk export for admins

Admins with full access can perform bulk exports across the system. This is useful for:

- Regular backups of organizational data
- Compliance audits
- Migrating to a different system

Bulk exports maintain encryption — the exported data remains protected at rest.

## Security of exports

All exports preserve the encryption of the original data. Files are only readable by people who have the correct device keys. Store export files securely and share them only through trusted channels.
